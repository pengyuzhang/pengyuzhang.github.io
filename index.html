Pengyu Zhang


I am a Senior Engineering Manager at Uber, where I lead teams in building large-scale, high-impact systems at the intersection of machine learning, distributed systems, and security applications. Previously, I was a Senior Engineering Manager and Staff Engineer at Alibaba Cloud in Seattle, where I drove the design and development of core infrastructure and cloud platform technologies. Before that, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Stanford University working with Sachin Katti. I earned my Ph.D. from UMass Amherst under the guidance of Deepak Ganesan, and my bachelor’s and master’s degrees from Tsinghua University.
